Bio

I'm passionate about the design process and working across it. From shaping the product vision and strategy all the way to refining all the details of a user flow to enhance usability. I will say that most of my most recent work has been in the front half of the double diamond design process. Conducting research to turn insights and early concept ideas into product priorities for cross-functional teams. In my next role, I hope to continue doing that and work on projects where we're iterating on existing experiences for maximum customer delight and ease of use. I want to continue developing my ability to deliver functional and usable digital products.

I thrive when I can work as a UX generalist and am excited to explore new industries I've never worked in before.
