Bio

I am passionate about the design process and learning from people with different approaches. My approach to design is centered around being deliberate about the problem we're solving and knowing whose experience we're touching, and why. I thrive when I'm able to shape the direction of an experience, based on the customer's goals, needs, and pain points. I enjoy bringing my product and engineering teammates through my design process, so they feel just as close to the customer as I do.
