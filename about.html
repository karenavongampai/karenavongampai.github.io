Skill set

Clarifying how business needs overlap with customer needs

I work closely with product and engineering to identify business goals and priorities. My role is to help businesses grow in alignment with what people want. I ground my teams in a deep understanding human behaviors, motivations, and goals.

Adapting my skillset to new problems and teammates

I'm comfortable wearing multiple hats, which has been useful when working across the design process in different problem spaces. I want to use my problem-solving skills to help people live happier, easier, and more sustainable lives.

In my experience the most effective way to share your perspective is to show the reasons that led you to your beliefs. And that doesn’t just mean having quantitative proof, but the complicated human story to explain why.
