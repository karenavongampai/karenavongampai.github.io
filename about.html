About me

Hi I’m Karena and I’m a product designer based in San Mateo, CA. My skill set is multifaceted, but I specialize in using research, product thinking, and interaction design to cut through ambiguity, assumptions and “gut feelings”. I take pride in being curious and listening intently to customers and my colleagues. I believe that the quality of my designs can only be as effective as how well I and my team understand the person using it and their underlying problems. Which is why I love getting my cross-functional teammates involved in my design/research process, so that they feel empowered to make informed decisions. I’ve held these values as a designer at every team I’ve joined.

I'm passionate about the design process and driving impact across it. I thrive when I can work as a UX generalist and get excited when I can apply my skills to new industries. In my next role, I hope to join a team with a strong culture of quality and curiosity.
