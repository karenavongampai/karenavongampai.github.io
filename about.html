Hi there! 👋

I’m a rock climber 🧗‍♀️ and product designer 👩‍💻.

With a passion for asking questions to learn about people and a collaborative spirit, I can cross-functionally create meaningful user experiences that balance the user’s needs against the business’.

Currently, I work on Intuit’s TurboTax, which helps helps ~30 million people do their taxes all by themselves. With prior experience as a User Research Intern at Amazon, I’m always the first person to ask why, and build empathy within my teams.

I received my Bachelors of Science in Human Centered Design & Engineering at the University of Washington in Seattle. I also grew up in the Bay Area, and have lived up and down the west coast.
